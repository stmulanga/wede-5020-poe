## Project Overview

The Bakery & Cafe is a modern bakery with three locations, offering freshly baked donuts, muffins, breads, cakes and drinks. The brand focuses on quality ingredients, daily freshness, and a warm café experience.

## Website Goals and Objectives

The primary goal of The Bakery & Cafe website is to promote its products and drive both online and in-store sales. The website aims to increase customer engagement by showcasing products and providing store information. Additionally, it seeks to build customer trust, encourage repeat visits and ensure a fast, user-friendly experience across all locations.
